Research Projects

Ice sheets and glaciers in a warming climate: measurement and modelling of surface melt lakes

2026–2029. This project aims to increase our understanding of how surface meltwater lakes form, evolve, and drain in a warming climate. We will study how varying atmospheric forcings and snowpack properties influence surface lake development, and investigate links between lake drainage and subglacial water storage potential.

This project is funded within the CROCUS Doctoral Landscape Award Scheme .



Tere Tīpako Tio: Rapid Extensive Ice Sampling Aotearoa

2022–2025. The goal of this project was to develop new low-cost, lightweight, easy-to-deploy ice sampling equipment for Antarctic boreholes.
